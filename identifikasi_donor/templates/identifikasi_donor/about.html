About the Application


The web-based AI system for Intelligent Blood Donor Identification has been developed to enhance efficiency and accuracy in the donor selection process. Utilizing advanced artificial intelligence algorithms, this system can quickly and accurately analyze and predict the eligibility status of prospective donors. This innovation aims to assist blood donation centers in optimizing the screening process and increasing the availability of safe blood for transfusions.

This AI system can recognize patterns and identify key factors that influence a person's eligibility to donate blood. By leveraging machine learning algorithms, the system can expedite the selection process and help reduce the risk of rejecting donors who do not meet the criteria. This improves the operational efficiency of blood donation centers and the quality of service to prospective donors.
